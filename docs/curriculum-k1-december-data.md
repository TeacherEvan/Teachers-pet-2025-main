**Grade:** K1/1

## English

**Activities:**

- ☐ Trace the letters following the dotted line and color the letters
- ☐ Draw lines from the letter to the picture starting from the dot
- ☐ Draw lines to match the same letter
- ☐ Draw lines to match the letter with the correct picture

**Vocabulary:** A-Ant, B-Bee, C-Cat, D-Dog, E-Egg, F-Fan, H-Hen, I-Ice Cream, O-Ox, T-Tea

## Mathematics

**Activities:**

- ☐ Write numbers 1-6 following the lines and write freehand in the box
- ☐ Draw the correct amount of circles in the box next to the numbers
- ☐ Trace number 6 following the dotted line and colour
- ☐ Write numbers 1-7 following the lines and write freehand in the box
- ☐ Draw the correct amount of circles in the box next to the number
- ☐ Color the correct amount of pictures and cut the pictures and paste them in the boxes

**Vocabulary:** One – ten

## I.Q (Intelligence/Logic)

**Activities:**

- ☐ Object and Animal - Circle the animals
- ☐ Direction (Above) - Cross the objects that are above
- ☐ Objects in the same group - Draw lines to match objects that are further
- ☐ Mark X to objects that are further
- ☐ Draw lines to match suitable size objects
- ☐ Direction: Front and back - Circle the pictures that face the front - Cross the pictures that face the back
- ☐ Animal (Wings) - Circle the animals that have wings
- ☐ Incomplete – Mark the incomplete objects with X

**Vocabulary:** Car, giraffe, girl, cat, telephone, alarm clock, vase/flower, toothbrush/toothpaste, pot/stove, bird, duck, hen, owl, lion, turtle, dog, book, boat, small girl, small dress, big girl, big dress, pencil, van, frog, rice cooker, swing, briefcase

## Science

**Activities:**

- ☐ Mixing colours - Students learn about primary and secondary colours by mixing coloured water
- ☐ Bottle diver experiment - Introduce K1 students to the concepts of buoyancy and density through a hands-on activity using a simple bottle and paper-clip
- ☐ Air pressure - Introduce K1 students to the concept of air pressure through a simple experiment using a candle and a bottle
- ☐ Air rocket - Introduce K1 students to the concept of air pressure of moving objects by pressure of a rocket toy

**Vocabulary:** Water, colour, mix, bowl, bottle, water, paper, paper-clip, food colouring, candle, air, sound, table, pencil, air rocket, stepping hard, press/push, fly up
