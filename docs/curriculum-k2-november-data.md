## I.Q
### Topics/Activities:
- Draw lines to match the shapes to a picture that uses all the shapes in 1st column
- Look at the 3 given pictures, and colour the one that is either the biggest or smallest
- Look at the given pictures and choose the pair of pictures that are the same size
- Put the given pictures in the correct order
- Draw lines to match the same activities
- Colour the longest object and shortest object
- Put an X on things going up and a slash on things going down
- Colour the heaviest and lightest object

### Vocabulary:
square, rectangle, triangle, circle, diamonds, penguin, kangaroo, House, big, small, colour, Kettle, Duck, Hexagon, Temple, Fans, Goose, Dolphins, Candle, watermelon, girl, Plant, Lotus, Chicken, boat, bath, Dance, Eat, read, red, blue, Truck, belt, paintbrush, Caterpillar, Slash, X, stairs, sled, turtle, walking, Lollipop, Chair, Flashlight, Ice cream, Plant, TV, House, slide, Milk, growth, order, up, down

### Topics/Activities:

## CONVERSATION 1
### Topics/Activities:
- Body Parts - Smell and Hear, Feel and see: What do you use to smell/hear/feel/see? -I use my nose/ears/hands/eyes
- How many ears/eyes/hands do you have? -I have two ears/eyes/hands
- Do you have 1 nose? -Yes I do
- What do you do with your hands/eyes? -I feel/see
- Where are you from? -I am from Thailand
- Common Action: What are you doing? -I am raising my hand
- Are you raising your hand? -Yes, I am
- Favourite Place: Where do you like to go? -I like to go to the zoo
- Do you like to go to the zoo? -Yes, I do

### Vocabulary:
Nose, Smell, Ears, Hear, Hands, Feel, Eyes, See, Senses, Thailand, raise, hand, school, park, zoo
