## MATHEMATICS
### Topics/Activities:
- Count from 1-10
- Trace numbers 1-5 following the dotted line and colouring the correct amount of pictures
- Write the correct numbers in the boxes next to the number (free hand)
- Draw the correct amount of circles in the box below or next to the number
- Color the correct amount of pictures
- Place the correct amount of pictures in the boxes
- Count the pictures and cross out each picture
- Counting from 1-10 with our fingers
- Draw lines to match the correct number to the pictures
